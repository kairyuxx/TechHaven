Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TechHaven

What is TechHaven?
TechHaven is your one-stop shop for the latest in electronics and gadgets, offering top-quality products ranging from smartphones and laptops to smart home devices and accessories.
Where are you located?
TechHaven operates 100% online. This allows us to offer competitive pricing and a wider product selection without the overhead of physical retail locations.
How long does delivery take?
Standard shipping takes 3–7 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.
Do your products come with a warranty?
Yes, all our products come with a standard manufacturer's warranty. Specific warranty terms vary by brand and product.
Is it safe to order online from TechHaven?
Yes, your privacy and security are our top priority. Our website uses SSL encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your data.
Can I return or exchange a product?
Yes, we offer a 14-day return and exchange policy for unused items in original packaging with proof of purchase. Some exclusions may apply.
Do you offer repairs or tech support?
We provide remote tech support, as well as repair services for selected devices. Visit our support page or contact us for details.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also log in to your account on our website to view your order status.
Can I cancel or change my order after placing it?
If your order hasn't shipped yet, you can request a change or cancellation by contacting us within 2 hours of purchase. After shipping, cancellation is not possible.
How can I contact customer service?

• Phone: +1 (800) 123-4567
• Email: support@techhaven.com
• Live chat on our website (available during business hours)
