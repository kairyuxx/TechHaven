TechHaven Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we protect your information.

What We Collect

  • Your name, email, phone number, and address when you place an order or contact us
  • Payment details to complete your purchases
  • Info about how you use our website (like pages visited and time spent)

How We Use It

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To contact you about your purchases
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send you updates or promotions (only if you agree)

We Don't Sell Your Info

We never sell your personal information. We only share it with trusted partners (like shipping companies and payment services) to complete your order.

Security

We protect your information with secure systems, but no system is 100% perfect. We do our best to keep your data safe.
